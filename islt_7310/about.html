About Me

I’m Billie, a photographer, graphic designer, amateur dj, and student at the University of Missouri in Columbia. Over the past year, I’ve started taking skate, street, product, and fashion photos. I hope to be a personal or fashion photographer in the future. I was born in Manila and moved to The States when I was six. Charlottesville, VA was my home for seven years, but I have been based in Columbia, MO since my dad became a professor at Mizzou in 2014.

While I started out doing photo and design for my friends, I am also a freelancer and do concert, individual, and brand-oriented content for others. I help bands, artists, and individuals look cool online - I’m on Instagram as mybloodyvirginiaave where you can find my work. In my free time, I like bass fishing, playing Apex, and being annoying and chronically online.
