Local Show
Photography

Columbia, MO

concert photography

The Columbia music scene is pretty good for a college town. Some of my friends are musicians and play in bands and I've had the opportunity to take photos of their live shows. The local bands and artists I've been able to capture include New Car Scent, Sifa, Ruby Lane, Catdad Jams, J. Artiz and the Soul Collective, Hang Your Hate, and The Blast Monkeys. Live shows are a fun environment to shoot, the performers and crowd both have energy that transfers well to photos.
